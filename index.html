Together We Can Save Lives

We work towards creating quality and equitable opportunities for children and communities to learn and grow with a view to long term sustainable change.NAS runs an initiative committed to reducing school dropout rates in children from backward families in rural areas of Bihar. Surveys revealed that lack of money to buy stationery, uniforms, textbooks and school fees is one of the primary reasons for dropping out of school. Those children are deprived of studies and start working to help their families.

About Us

NAS is an NGO working since 2020 in the fields of Social, Rural, Health, Education, Environment, Agriculture, Women and Child Development. We are located in a remote village in Bihar which works for poor people. Our overall goal is the empowerment of people from poor and marginalized communities, thereby improving their lives and livelihoods. We are the architects of the government vision of sustainable development under this umbrella, we have organized various development projects, workshops, training camps and programs etc.
